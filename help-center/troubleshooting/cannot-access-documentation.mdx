---
title: Cannot Access Documentation
description: Resolve issues loading project documentation.
---

## Symptoms

- Documentation pages don't load
- Error: "Access Denied"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your login status">
    Log in again at `https://stepin-docs.com/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Ensure you have project access (ask the owner if unsure).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try incognito or clear cache">
    Sometimes browser cache can cause issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
