## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Project">
    Click "New Project" on the dashboard and give it a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Documentation Pages">
    Use the "Add Page" button to create your first document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collaborate">
    Invite team members by email in the project settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
